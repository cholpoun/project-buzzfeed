Top 5 fascinating facts about dinosaurs

Dinosaurs Dominated Earth for Over 160 Million Years

Dinosaurs roamed the Earth for over 160 million years, and their reign began about 230 million years ago. The T-Rex was one of the largest meat-eating dinosaurs, standing up to 40 feet tall.


They Came in All Shapes and Sizes

Dinosaurs ranged from the size of a chicken, like the Compsognathus, to the massive Argentinosaurus,which could reach up to 100 feet in length and weigh around 100 tons. This diversity in ssize allowed them to occupy various ecological niches.

Birds Are Modern-Day Dinosaurs

Scientific research has shown that birds are actually the direct descendants of theropod dinosaurs, like the Velociraptor. This means that dinosaurs are not entirely extinctâ€”rather, they live on as birds

Some Dinosaurs Had Feathers

Contrary to the classic image of scaly, reptilian dinosaurs, many species, especially theropods, had feathers. These feathers might have been used for insulation, display, or even primitive forms of flights

The Tyrannosaurus rex Had an Exceptional Sense of Smell

The T. rex, one of the most iconic dinosaurs, had a highly developed olfactory region in its brain, indicating that it relied heavily on its sense of smell to hunt and scavenge.
