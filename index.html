Top 11 fascinating facts about dinosaurs

Dinosaurs Dominated Earth for Over 160 Million Years

Dinosaurs roamed the Earth for over 160 million years, and their reign began about 230 million years ago.

The T-Rex was one of the largest meat-eating dinosaurs, standing up to 40 feet tall.


They Came in All Shapes and Sizes

Dinosaurs ranged from the size of a chicken, like the Compsognathus, to the massive Argentinosaurus,which could reach up to 100 feet in length and weigh around 100 tons.

This diversity in ssize allowed them to occupy various ecological niches.
